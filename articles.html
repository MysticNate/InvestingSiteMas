Learn your way to success

Understanding the basics of investing is a crucial step towards securing your financial future.

Whether you're just starting or looking to expand your knowledge, these websites offer valuable insights and resources to help you navigate the world of investing.

From learning the fundamentals to mastering advanced strategies, these platforms cater to individuals at every stage of their investing journey.

Explore these sites to gain the knowledge and confidence needed to make informed investment decisions.

Now, let's dive into three different websites that can teach you about basic investing:

1) A 15 page document from the Berkeley University: Berkeley Uni - Investing101

2) Basic investing in stocks from the Washington Department of Financial Institutions: Washington - What are stocks?

3) What are indexes and all you need to know about them from the S&P Global: S&P Global - All about indexes
