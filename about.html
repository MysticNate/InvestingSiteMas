About Us

Meet Giora, Nati, and Waseem, three individuals whose unconventional paths led them to found the successful investment firm GoodNight Investing.
Their stories highlight the power of determination, ingenuity, and risk-taking in the world of finance.

Our Team

Nati Masfin

UFO & Founder

Meet Nati, a high school student who spent more time in detention than in class.
Bored during his many hours of detention, Nati started tinkering with the school computers.
He quickly discovered a talent for hacking and began exploring the depths of the internet, learning programming languages and honing his skills.
Using his newfound abilities, Nati started hacking into various systems, including his school's grading system to improve his grades.
Word of Nati's skills spread, and he soon found himself in high demand from people seeking his hacking expertise.
Instead of using his skills for nefarious purposes, Nati decided to start a cybersecurity company, using his knowledge to help protect others from cyber threats.
Today, Nati is a renowned programmer and cybersecurity expert, known for his ingenuity and skill.

Giora Glovatsky

CEO & Founder

Giora grew up in the depths of the Russian forest, living off the land and riding his trusty bear, Boris.
One day, while wandering through the forest, Giora stumbled upon a broken laptop discarded by a passing hiker.
Intrigued, Giora took the laptop back to his humble abode and, with the help of some online tutorials, managed to fix it using spare parts he found in the forest.
With his newfound access to the digital world, Giora began to learn about investing.
He started small, investing in local businesses and stocks.
To everyone's surprise, Giora had a knack for predicting market trends and making profitable investments.
As his wealth grew, Giora expanded his investments globally, becoming a billionaire and earning the nickname "The Bear Baron" for his unique approach to investing.

Waseem Masalha

CFO & Founder

Waseem grew up in Dubai, surrounded by wealth and luxury.
From a young age, Waseem was exposed to the world of finance through his family's oil trading business.
By the time he was in college, Waseem was already a seasoned trader, with a keen eye for market trends and a knack for making profitable trades.
Using his family's resources and his own expertise, Waseem never made a losing trade.
He had a reputation for being able to predict market movements with uncanny accuracy, earning him the nickname "The Oil Sultan."
Today, Waseem's trading empire spans the globe, and he is considered one of the most successful traders in the world, thanks to his early start and unwavering determination.
